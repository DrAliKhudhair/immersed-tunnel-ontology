Immersed Tunnel Ontology - Facilities Systems (Temporary and Permanent): Description back to ToC

Consolidated facilities systems for immersed tunnel projects including temporary construction facilities and permanent operational facilities. CONSOLIDATES: - Dry dock facilities from dry_dock_facilities.owl - Auxiliary works systems from auxiliary_works_systems.owl - Temporary construction facilities - Permanent operational facilities - Support infrastructure
