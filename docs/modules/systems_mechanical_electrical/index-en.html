Immersed Tunnel Ontology - Mechanical and Electrical Systems: Description back to ToC

Consolidated mechanical and electrical systems for immersed tunnels. Includes ventilation, lighting (excluding emergency), power supply, control, and communication systems. Based on GB/T 51318-2019 and JTG 2120-2020 standards. CONSOLIDATES: - Ventilation systems from mechanical_electrical_systems.owl - Normal lighting systems from mechanical_electrical_systems.owl - Electrical systems from ancillary_structures_traffic_engineering.owl - Control systems from ancillary_structures_traffic_engineering.owl EXCLUDES: - Emergency lighting (moved to systems_safety_emergency.owl) - Fire protection systems (moved to systems_safety_emergency.owl) - Drainage systems (moved to systems_drainage_utilities.owl)
