Immersed Tunnel Ontology - Structural Systems and Components: Description back to ToC

Enhanced structural systems and components for immersed tunnels. Comprehensive structural design, joints, and reinforcement systems. Based on GB/T 51318-2019 and JTG 2120-2020 Chapter 7-8 standards. CONSOLIDATES: - Structural components from structural_components.owl - Joint systems from joint_systems.owl - Reinforcement and prestressing systems - Structural connections and details ENHANCEMENTS: - Advanced structural analysis - Optimized joint design - Smart monitoring integration - Durability enhancement
