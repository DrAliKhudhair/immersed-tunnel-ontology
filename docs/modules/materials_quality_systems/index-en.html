Immersed Tunnel Ontology - Materials and Quality Systems: Description back to ToC

Enhanced materials and quality control systems for immersed tunnels. Comprehensive material specifications, testing, and quality assurance. Based on GB/T 51318-2019 and JTG 2120-2020 materials standards. CONSOLIDATES: - Material specifications from material_specifications.owl - Quality control from quality_control.owl - Testing procedures and standards - Material properties and performance ENHANCEMENTS: - Advanced material characterization - Smart quality monitoring - Digital material tracking - Performance optimization
