Immersed Tunnel Ontology - Safety and Emergency Systems: Description back to ToC

Consolidated safety and emergency systems for immersed tunnels. Unifies fire protection, emergency response, evacuation, and safety monitoring systems. Based on GB/T 51318-2019 and JTG 2120-2020 standards. CONSOLIDATES: - Fire protection from mechanical_electrical_systems.owl and ancillary_structures_traffic_engineering.owl - Emergency systems from auxiliary_works_systems.owl - Safety monitoring from multiple modules REPLACES: - Fire protection parts of mechanical_electrical_systems.owl - Emergency parts of auxiliary_works_systems.owl - Safety parts of ancillary_structures_traffic_engineering.owl
