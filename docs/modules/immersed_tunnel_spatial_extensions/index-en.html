Immersed Tunnel Engineering - Spatial Extensions: Description back to ToC

Focused extensions for immersed tunnel engineering terminology and spatial concepts. Covers specific immersed tunnel requirements for: 1. Immersed tunnel-specific spatial elements (segments, elements, joints, envelopes) 2. Non-physical zones specific to immersed tunnel operations 3. Immersed tunnel lifecycle and asset management 4. Prefabricated element management and tracking 5. Immersion-specific project metadata and processes This module focuses exclusively on immersed tunnel engineering terminology.
