Immersed Tunnel Ontology - Engineering Design and Analysis: Description back to ToC

Enhanced engineering design and analysis systems for immersed tunnels. Comprehensive design processes, analysis methods, and engineering evaluation. Based on GB/T 51318-2019 and JTG 2120-2020 standards. CONSOLIDATES: - Design processes from design_processes.owl - Engineering analysis from engineering_analysis.owl - Design requirements and specifications - Technical schemes and methods ENHANCEMENTS: - Integrated design workflow management - Advanced analysis capabilities - Design optimization methods - Validation and verification processes

Static Analysisc back to ToC or Class ToC

IRI: http://www.semanticweb.org/immersedTunnelOnto#StaticAnalysis

Static structural analysis implementing GBT 51318-2019 Section 8.3 pipe section force calculations. Transverse direction calculations per Section 8.3.1 segmented by water depth and soil cover using plane strain models considering: water pressure, soil cover load, lateral earth pressure, lateral water pressure, internal load and ballast. Longitudinal direction calculations per Section 8.3.2 consider structure shape, construction methods, wave forces, and flow velocity. During construction uses 3D numerical simulations for complex cases. During operation employs elastic foundation beam models. Analysis incorporates uneven settlement, temperature change, concrete shrinkage, and joint deformations. Historical soil bearing capacity and construction process data used for settlement estimation.
