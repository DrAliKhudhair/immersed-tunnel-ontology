Immersed Tunnel Ontology - Drainage and Utilities Systems: Description back to ToC

Consolidated drainage and utilities systems for immersed tunnels. Unifies all drainage, water supply, pumping, and utility systems. Based on GB/T 51318-2019 and JTG 2120-2020 standards. CONSOLIDATES: - DrainageSystem from mechanical_electrical_systems.owl - TunnelDrainageSystem hierarchy from foundation_systems_components.owl - DrainageSystem from dry_dock_facilities.owl - Water supply systems from ancillary_structures_traffic_engineering.owl UNIFIES: - All drainage system hierarchies into single coherent structure - Pumping systems and equipment - Water supply and distribution systems - Utility infrastructure systems
