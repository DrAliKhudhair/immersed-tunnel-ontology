Immersed Tunnel Ontology - Site and Environmental Systems: Description back to ToC

Site characterization and environmental systems module for immersed tunnel engineering aligned with GBT 51318-2019. Defines comprehensive framework for site investigation, environmental assessment, and monitoring systems. STANDARDS ALIGNMENT: - GBT 51318-2019 Chapter 4: Engineering Survey and Site Investigation methodology - Section 4.3: Phased tunnel investigation framework and requirements - Section 4.2.2: Comprehensive survey data type specifications - Investigation phases: feasibility study, preliminary, detailed, and supplemental INVESTIGATION FRAMEWORK: - Phased investigation approach matching design stages - Subaqueous topographic mapping with scale-specific requirements - Water area investigation with systematic borehole arrangements - Hydrological investigation including flow velocity and water characteristics - Integration of underwater and terrestrial survey methodologies MODULE SCOPE: - Site investigation classification and methodology framework - Environmental assessment and monitoring system taxonomy - Survey data type definitions aligned with standard requirements - Environmental protection and sustainability system architecture - Design parameter derivation from investigation data
