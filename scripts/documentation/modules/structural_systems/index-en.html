Immersed Tunnel Ontology - Structural Systems Module: Description back to ToC

Structural systems module providing comprehensive classification and specifications for immersed tunnel structural design. Aligned with GBT 51318-2019 "Immersed Tunnel Design Standard" covering structural design, joint systems, and foundation integration. This module encompasses: - Settlement-based joint design classes for IMMTD05 - Longitudinal structural system design for IMMTD06 - Tunnel segment geometry and specifications - Joint design and waterproofing requirements - Foundation integration requirements STANDARDS ALIGNMENT: - Structural design methodology per GBT 51318-2019 - Joint design requirements and specifications - Segment division logic and optimization
