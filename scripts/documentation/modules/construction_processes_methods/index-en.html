Immersed Tunnel Ontology - Construction Processes and Methods: Description back to ToC

Construction processes and methods module for immersed tunnel systems aligned with GBT 51318-2019. Defines the systematic construction methodology and procedural framework for immersed tunnel construction. STANDARDS ALIGNMENT: - GBT 51318-2019: Construction methodology and terminology reference - JTG 2120-2020: Highway tunnel construction procedures - Construction stages follow systematic phasing requirements - Process definitions align with standard construction terminology PROCESS DEFINITIONS: - Floating transportation: Process of buoyant transport to installation position - Immersion: Controlled descent process to designated underwater position - Connection: Structural joining process between tunnel elements - Overall methodology framework for systematic construction execution MODULE SCOPE: - Construction process classification and hierarchy - Construction stage definitions and sequencing - Construction method taxonomy and procedures - Quality control and safety management frameworks
