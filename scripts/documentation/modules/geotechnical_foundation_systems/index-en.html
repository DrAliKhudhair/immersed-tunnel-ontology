Immersed Tunnel Ontology - Geotechnical and Foundation Systems: Description back to ToC

Enhanced geotechnical analysis and foundation systems for immersed tunnels. Comprehensive soil analysis, foundation design, and ground improvement. Based on GB/T 51318-2019 and JTG 2120-2020 Chapter 9-10 standards. CONSOLIDATES: - Geotechnical analysis from geotechnical_analysis.owl - Foundation systems from foundation_systems_components.owl - Soil investigation and characterization - Ground improvement and treatment methods ENHANCEMENTS: - Advanced geotechnical modeling - Foundation optimization - Ground treatment strategies - Stability analysis methods
