Immersed Tunnel Ontology - Monitoring and Instrumentation Systems: Description back to ToC

Enhanced monitoring and instrumentation systems for immersed tunnels. Comprehensive monitoring including structural health, environmental conditions, and safety systems. Based on GB/T 51318-2019 and JTG 2120-2020 Chapter 16 standards. ENHANCED FROM: unified_monitoring_systems.owl CONSOLIDATES: - Structural monitoring systems - Environmental monitoring systems - Risk analysis and data acquisition - Real-time monitoring and alerting
