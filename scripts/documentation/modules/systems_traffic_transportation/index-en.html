Immersed Tunnel Ontology - Traffic and Transportation Systems: Description back to ToC

Consolidated traffic and transportation systems for immersed tunnels. Includes traffic engineering facilities, safety systems, signs, barriers, and transportation infrastructure. Based on GB/T 51318-2019 and JTG 2120-2020 Chapter 15 standards. CONSOLIDATES: - Traffic engineering facilities from ancillary_structures_traffic_engineering.owl - Traffic safety systems from ancillary_structures_traffic_engineering.owl - Transportation infrastructure and access systems - Traffic monitoring and control systems EXCLUDES: - Emergency lighting and fire systems (moved to systems_safety_emergency.owl) - Electrical power systems (moved to systems_mechanical_electrical.owl) - Monitoring systems (moved to systems_monitoring_instrumentation.owl)
